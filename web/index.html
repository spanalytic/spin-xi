📖 How to Play

1 · Spin & pick your XI

  1. Hit Play on the live round and choose a formation.
  2. SPIN the reel — it lands on a random team. Teams can come up more than once.
  3. Pick any player from that team — they fill the next open slot for their position (GK / DEF / MID / FWD). Each player can only be picked once.
  4. Nobody fits your remaining slots? Spin again.
  5. Repeat until your XI is complete.

2 · Captain & enter

  1. Tap a player on the pitch to make them captain — they score double points (negatives too!). You can change your mind until you enter the team.
  2. Enter my Team saves it and posts it to the 🏆 Leaderboard under your name (set your name on the Leaderboard tab first).
  3. Enter as many teams as you like — every team goes on the board.
  4. Points appear as the real matches finish — ⏳ means games are still to be played and the score will keep updating.
